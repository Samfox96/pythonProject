About Django Weather Services

Hi There, Django Weather Services App here. We are a new player in the virtual weather game. We aim to offer on the spot updates for all things weather wherever you are in the world. We have a strong desire to deliver the best possible experience to our users so please let us know through our customer feedback app if there is any changes you see fit for us.


Our patented and highly advanced weather satellite gives us millisecond updates for weather systems worldwide which allows us to not only provide more accurate weather readings but a broader range of where we are able to proivde reports from. Currently 100% of the globe.


And just for fun our next project will be able to tell you the up to date current temperature of the moon! However let's keep that one a secret between you and us as we are not quite done yet
